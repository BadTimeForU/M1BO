Mijn top 10 favorite songs

1. “Red Sun in the Sky” (太阳红): This one is an absolute classic. The repetitive chorus of “Xue hua piao piao, bei feng xiao xiao” has become iconic. It’s like a soothing mantra for anyone who wants to feel calm while imagining snowflakes drifting and winds whispering. 2. “Xue Hua Piao Piao” (雪花飘飘): Yes, this is the same phrase from the previous song! It’s actually a snippet from a Chinese drama, and it became a meme because of its dreamy and poetic quality. People love to use it in various contexts, from romantic moments to everyday life. 3. “Chin Cheng Hanji” (秦城寒暨): This one has a mysterious vibe. The lyrics talk about a cold city (秦城) and longing (寒暨). It’s hauntingly beautiful and has inspired countless memes and parodies. 4. “Without the Communist Party, There Would Be No New China” (没有共产党就没有新中国): This song is a remix of a speech by Chinese leader Xi Jinping. The catchy beat and the message make it memorable. It’s both serious and amusing at the same time. 5. “Xuanzang” (玄奘): Named after the famous Buddhist monk Xuanzang, who traveled to India to collect Buddhist scriptures, this song is a blend of history and humor. It’s a bit quirky and definitely meme-worthy.
